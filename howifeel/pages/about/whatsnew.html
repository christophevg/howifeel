What's new?

This little project grows, little by little new features get added. On this page we'll track these features as they get added...

Gravatars are here

We recently introduced your personal navigation menu. Today we added support for gravatars. Visit your settings page to add your email and if you also have a gravatar... it will popup.

Following

Publicly accessible mood URLs are a cornerstone of How I Feel. It allows you to share with anyone, not requiring them to create an account of their own.

As of now, if they have an account and are logged in when visiting your public mood URL, they'll have the option to "follow you" and your mood will be added to their following page.
